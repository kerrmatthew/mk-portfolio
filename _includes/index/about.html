About Me

I'm a UX Designer at Trade Me, the biggest website in New Zealand.

I have a Masters of Entreprenurship from Otago, and a Bachelor of Design from Victoria focusing on Architecture, so naturally I've spent the last 7 years building products for mobile and web.

I'm pretty good at digital design and building products. I've spun up and killed more startups than I care to admit. And now I'm learning all I can in the corporate world.

If I had to describe my work – and I do since this is my portfolio – I'd say I'm honest, easy to work with, proactive, and focused on users. My communication and organisation skills are top notch, when they need to be, albeit a bit casual at times. I grew up in New Zealand after all.
I am most comfortable as a contributor to a larger team with shared vision. I know when to put the extra efford in to get the outcomes we need.
