About Me

I'm a Designer at Trade Me, the unofficial website of New Zealand.

With a Masters degree in business, and a Design Bachelor focusing on Architecture, it doesn't really make a whole lot of sense that I've spent the last 7 years building products for mobile and web.

Nevertheless, I'm pretty good at digital design and building products. I've spun up and killed more startups than I care to admit, and the one I did managed to sell basically scared me off owning a business. And here you find me in a corporate.

If I had to describe my work, and I do since this is my portfolio, I'd say I'm honest, easy to work with, proactive, and focused on users. My communication and organisation skills are top notch, albeit a bit casual at times. I like to think of myself as an important contributor to a larger team with shared vision; I thrive in a positive environment. I also know when to put the extra efford in to get the outcomes we need.
